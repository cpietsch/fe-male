Visualization in Journalism

M100 Young European Journalists Workshop
8./11. September 2014 at University of Applied
Sciences Potsdam

During two intense and fruitful workshop days the University of Applied
Sciences Potsdam in cooperation with the M100 Sanssouci Colloquium
gave a hands-on introduction to visual storytelling in data-driven journalism
for 24 young journalists from all over Europe. This site presents the
results of the workshop.

Women in politics

Did you ever get a feeling that men rule the world? How true is it actually and if it is true - why are there so very few females among politicians? Before responding why, it is crucial to actually come up with the numbers and see how accurate is the statement on women underrepresentation.
